Product Data Exports for SKF Authorized Distributors

Purchasing behaviours are changing. Customers are on a large scale, searching for and buying products online. Make sure you provide your customers with the up-to-date product information they are looking for. Through our Product Data Exports, you can get access to constantly updated product information for web shops and e-commerce systems.

The exports are available in Excel and XML formats

▪ Contents : Product attributes, text description, Links to product photos and SKF Online Product Catalogue.
▪ Some product attributes are valid only to a limited number of products – these are in the Excel-format visible as empty cells.
▪ ZIP archive is available for product photos.

Assortments for product data exports(Based on European reference price list)
▪ SKF Bearings
▪ SKF Seals
▪ SKF Maintenance Products
▪ SKF Power Transmission Products

Available languages
English, Chinese, Dutch, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Russian, Spanish, Danish, Polish
